Privacy Policy

At Aaai Solutions, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

Information Collection and Use

We collect various types of information in connection with the services we provide, including:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, and other relevant details as necessary).
  • Additional information: Depending on the nature of the project, we may request further details to ensure the solution meets your needs.

Use of Data

Aaai Solutions uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services: Your information is crucial for us to deliver and support our solutions.
  • To notify you about changes to our services: We keep you informed about updates or modifications to our services.
  • To provide customer support: Your details help us address your queries and provide the necessary support.
  • To monitor the usage of our services: We analyze how our services are used to improve and innovate.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues: Ensuring the smooth operation of our services requires understanding potential problems.

Disclosure of Data

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. All information collected is strictly for internal use to enhance your experience and the effectiveness of our services.

Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
